# Meraki House Brand Guidelines

**Version:** 1.0

---

# 1. Brand Identity

## Brand Name

**Meraki House**

---

## Meaning

The word **Meraki** means doing something with soul, creativity, and love.

Every product should communicate craftsmanship, sustainability, and care.

---

## Industry

Organic Haircare & Bodycare

---

## Brand Position

A modern organic self-care brand focused on handmade products, sustainable ingredients, and premium everyday essentials.

---

# 2. Brand Personality

Meraki House should feel:

* Warm
* Calm
* Honest
* Organic
* Premium
* Handmade
* Sustainable
* Elegant
* Trustworthy
* Minimal

The brand should never feel:

* Loud
* Flashy
* Cheap
* Corporate
* Overcrowded
* Artificial

---

# 3. Brand Voice

Every piece of content should sound:

* Friendly
* Calm
* Informative
* Genuine
* Encouraging
* Minimal

Examples:

✔ Crafted with care.

✔ Nourish your hair naturally.

✔ Gentle ingredients. Powerful results.

✖ BUY NOW!!!

✖ LIMITED OFFER!!!

✖ CHEAPEST PRICE!!

---

# 4. Brand Mission

To provide handcrafted organic haircare and bodycare products that encourage mindful self-care while promoting sustainable living.

---

# 5. Brand Values

* Sustainability
* Transparency
* Natural Ingredients
* Cruelty-Free Practices
* Quality over Quantity
* Customer Wellbeing
* Minimal Environmental Impact

---

# 6. Target Audience

### Primary

* College students
* Young professionals
* Women & men interested in natural self-care
* Eco-conscious shoppers

### Age Group

18–40 years

---

* FAQs

## Supporting Fonts

### Raleway Thin

Use for:

* Decorative Numbers
* Background Typography
* Editorial Elements

### Raleway ExtraLight

Use for:

* Captions
* Small Labels
* Secondary Information

# 9. Logo Usage

The logo should always have sufficient whitespace.

Never:

* Stretch the logo
* Rotate it
* Add shadows
* Add outlines
* Change colors

Preferred placement:

* Navbar
* Footer
* Login
* Packaging Mockups

---

# 10. Favicon

Use the official Meraki House favicon.

Minimum size:

32 × 32 px

---

# 11. Iconography

Source:

**The Noun Project**

Style:

* Outline Icons
* Thin Stroke
* Rounded Corners
* Minimal
* Monochrome

Never mix:

* Filled icons
* 3D icons
* Cartoon icons

---

# 12. Illustration Style

Illustrations should be:

* Organic
* Minimal
* Flat
* Warm
* Handcrafted
* Soft Color Palette

Avoid:

* Neon colors
* Heavy gradients
* Cartoonish styles

---

# 13. Photography Style

Photography should include:

* Natural Lighting
* Wooden Textures
* Stone Surfaces
* Cotton Fabrics
* Ceramic Accessories
* Plants
* Neutral Backgrounds

Products should feel handcrafted and authentic.

---

# 14. Design Language

Overall design style:

**Modern Minimal Bohemian**

Characteristics:

* Large Whitespace
* Rounded Corners
* Soft Shadows
* Calm Layouts
* Editorial Feel
* Organic Composition

---

# 15. UI Principles

Every screen should prioritize:

* Simplicity
* Readability
* Consistency
* Accessibility
* Performance

Avoid visual clutter.

Use spacing to create hierarchy instead of excessive borders or colors.

---

# 16. Animations

Animations should be:

* Smooth
* Slow
* Subtle

Preferred transitions:

* Fade
* Scale
* Slide

Avoid:

* Bounce
* Flash
* Shake
* Overly energetic effects

---

# 17. Future Brand Extensions

Possible future additions:

* Gift Boxes
* Seasonal Collections
* Haircare Kits
* Subscription Boxes
* Limited Edition Products
* Blog
* Loyalty Program
* Referral Program

---

# 18. Brand Consistency Checklist

Before adding any new page or component, verify:

* Uses approved typography.
* Uses approved colors.
* Matches the minimal bohemian style.
* Uses consistent iconography.
* Maintains generous whitespace.
* Supports responsive layouts.
* Follows accessibility best practices.
* Aligns with the overall Meraki House identity.

---

## Brand Summary

Meraki House represents calm, handcrafted, sustainable self-care.

Every interaction should make users feel that the products are thoughtfully made, environmentally conscious, and premium without being extravagant.
