# Home & Living Category Asset Guidelines

This document outlines the organized folder structure, naming conventions, and asset standards for the Home & Living category.

---

# Folder Structure

## Category-Level Folders

*   **`hero/`**: Main category header banners.
    *   **`hero/desktop/`**: Desktop banners (16:5 or 3:1).
    *   **`hero/mobile/`**: Mobile banners (3:4 or 9:16).
*   **`banners/`**: Campaign, promotional, and seasonal banners.
*   **`thumbnails/`**: Images for lists, collection grids, and cart item previews.
*   **`icons/`**: Badges, materials certifications, or category-specific symbols.
*   **`illustrations/`**: Custom sketches, vectors, and lifestyle diagrams.
*   **`lifestyle/`**: Styled photography showcasing product application and context.
*   **`ingredients/`**: High-quality closeups of natural ingredients (e.g., soy wax, essential oils).
*   **`videos/`**: Educational videos, tutorials, and category reels.
*   **`collection/`**: Curated product group assets or composite category layout graphics.
*   **`gift-kit/`**: Styling, layout, and packaging assets for Home & Living gift sets.

## Product-Level Folders (`products/[product-name]/`)

Every individual product folder under `products/` is structured with these folders:
*   **`front/`**: Primary product image showing the front of the packaging or product bar/bottle, isolated on a clean background.
*   **`back/`**: Product image showing the back of the packaging (barcode, ingredients, warnings).
*   **`angle/`**: 3D perspective product shots showing side, top, or three-quarter views.
*   **`flatlay/`**: Creative flatlay/top-down arrangements featuring the product with props or raw ingredients.
*   **`lifestyle/`**: Contextual or modeled photography featuring the product in a real-world setting.
*   **`videos/`**: How-to-use videos, texture demonstrations, or close-up reels.
